I am currently a preliminary medicine student at UC Riverside, majoring in psychology to broaden my understanding of human beings in general. This academic pursuit aims to enhance my skills as a future physician, enabling me to provide compassionate care to patients. Additionally, I am the founder of several organizations, both affiliated with UCR and operating independently as NGOs. Some of these initiatives were established to align with my interest in medicine, particularly psychiatry. Moreover, one of the organizations was created to support first-generation students, like myself, in successfully navigating their career pathways in medicine and various healthcare fields.

Here are a few of the organizations I have created and currently lead:

  • A Daily Dose of Medicine is a nonprofit initiative I co-founded with my computer science friends—one from UCSD and the other one from Korea University.
  • OCAPUCR © was founded to offer free mentorship programs, aiming to assist pre-medical, dental, and pharmacy students in acquiring valuable information to prepare for their future careers.
  • You Deserve to be Loved was founded to deliver therapeutic messages and encouragement, contributing to the improvement of our UCR campus community.

Organization:

A Daily Dose of Medicine

This company is dedicated to provide the audience with short and quick information about some of the exciting aspects of medicine and biomedical science in general through a notification app. I led and directed a group of talented engineers through the final stages of building the app. Additionally, I plan to offer up to 70 internship positions to pre-health students who are interested in becoming our general editor interns. This initiative is designed to provide them with opportunities to gain valuable internship experiences.

OCAPUCR ©

This organization is dedicated to assist pre-medical, dental, and pharmacy students in obtaining valuable information to prepare for their future careers. I have successfully guided more than 75 students to succeed in their bachelor and post-bachelor careers, utilizing various resources. These resources include a mini book series, free tutoring sessions for classes, workshops to find shadowing positions, and conferences aimed at enhancing understanding of the matriculation process for medical schools and preparing the application.

You Deserve to be Loved

This organization is dedicated to delivering therapeutic messages and encouragement to students who request them through our Google form. We have provided support to more than 20 students, contributing to the improvement of our UCR campus community by offering an online sanctuary during their most challenging times.
